FRIED RICE

Heads Up

We are long overdue for me to share my easy, classic Fried Rice recipe. This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese. This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Veggies
  • Spices
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Cook meat with spices and veggies
  2. Add Rice
  3. Add egg
  4. Mix well
  5. Add more spices
